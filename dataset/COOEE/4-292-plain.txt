


She has great times paying and receiving visits as she mooves in the upper ten, as I call them, the are all so fine. I do the rough Irish man, by the way, and takes sum of the down about their language. The seem to know so much in one generation by their forefathers as they were clumsey allowing themselves to be catched thieving and then getting a free passage and rashings. The don't like it for the are realy good looking and smart.
