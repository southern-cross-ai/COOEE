


if I had my liberty I would not go home for three or four years so I request of you to prepare yourself and come out as soon as you can for I can make as much money in three or four years as would support me without working whilst I live  I am in a factuary making soap and candles since I came to the Country and as happy as I please. I don't expect that my son John will be let with you so send him in the prison man ship  fail not to come for you will be as happy as you were in all your life  Dont forget bringing Peggy out  Tell my brother John to come if he can.
