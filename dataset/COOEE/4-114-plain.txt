


I notice by the Melbourne papers that the Protestants of the North of Ireland are arming themselves with all possible haste, in prospect of the Home Rule Bill passing. I suppose it is the Orangemen principally who are making this ado. I don't think the Home Rule Bill can possibly be passed. I am sorry Mr. Gladstone made such a mistake. I believe it would be a bad thing for the Protestants if it was to become law.
