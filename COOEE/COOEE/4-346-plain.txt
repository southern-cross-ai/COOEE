


Government House,
Melbourne, Dec. 8th, 1896.
Dear Sir, 
I am desired by His Excellency, Lord Brassey, to acknowledge the receipt of your letter of the 28rd, and to ask you to convey his thanks to the members of the People's Federal Convention, for the Address which they have so kindly forwarded to him. His Excellency is much gratified to hear that the letter which he addressed to the Convention was so much appreciated.
I have the honour to be,
Your obedient Servant,
RICHARD NEVILL
